## Question 27
As of 1 May 2018, Alan owned 1,000 units of Fund MNO with an NAV price per unit of RM2.00. A distribution of RM0.40 per unit was declared and paid in the form of units. Immediately after the distribution payment, the NAV price per unit of Fund MNO was reduced to RM1.60.

**What is the total number of units Alan owns after the distribution?**

**Options:**
- A. 800 units
- B. 250 units
- C. 1,000 units
- D. 1,250 units

**Calculation:**
- Distribution = 1,000 × RM0.40 = RM400
- Units from distribution = RM400 / RM1.60 = 250 units
- Total units = 1,000 + 250 = 1,250 units

- A. 5,000 units
- B. 3,800 units
- C. 3,500 units
- D. 4,000 units

- DEF → based on changes in NAV/U, units double from 1,000 units increased to 2,000 units
