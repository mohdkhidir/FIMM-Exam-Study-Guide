## Question 3 of 60
**Fund XYZ recently launched a fund on 3 January 20X2 at RM 0.50 NAV per unit with sales charge of 3.5%. Bob invested RM1,000 in the fund on 30 January 20X2 at 10pm. Details of the fund's pricing are shown below:**

| Date | NAV per unit |
|------|-------------|
| 30 January 20X2 | RM 0.76 |
| 31 January 20X2 | RM 0.80 |

**Assuming that fund XYZ adopts forward pricing and the dealing cut-off point is at 4pm every business day, what would be the number of units Bob will get?**

**Options:**
- A. 773 units
- B. 733 units
- C. 760 units
- D. 800 units

**Calculation:**
- Net investment amount (working money) = RM1,000 / 1.035 = RM966.18
- No of units (forward pricing, will be RM0.80) = RM966.18 / RM0.80 = 1,207.73 units

## Question 18 of 60
**Below is the particulars of Cindy's investment holding in a UTS/PRS as at 1 June 2020:**

- (a) 2,000 units
- (b) NAV per unit: RM1.50
- (c) A distribution of RM0.50 per unit was declared and paid on the same date in the form of fund units.

**How many number of units did Cindy own after the distribution?**

**Options:**
- A. 1,000 units
- B. 2,000 units
- C. 3,000 units (CORRECT)
- D. 2,500 units

**Calculation:**
- Distribution = 2,000 units × RM0.50 = RM1,000
- NAV per unit after distribution = RM1.50 – RM0.50 = RM1.00
- Units to be issued from the distribution = RM1,000 / RM1.00 = 1,000
- Total units = 2,000 units + 1,000 units = 3,000 units
