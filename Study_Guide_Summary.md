## Important Formulas and Calculations

### NAV Calculation
```
NAV = Total Assets - Total Liabilities
NAV per Unit = Total NAV / Number of Units
Unit Price = Net Assets / Units in Circulation
```

### Investment Calculations
```
Working Money = Capital / (1 + Service Charge)
Number of Units = Working Money / NAV per Unit
Redemption Proceeds = Number of Units × NAV per Unit
Dollar Cost Averaging = Total Investment / Total Units
```

### Performance Metrics
```
Raw Return = (End Price - Begin Price) / Begin Price × 100%
Compound Annual Return: FV = PV(1 + i)^n
Rule of 72: 72 / Interest Rate = Years (or vice versa)
Management Expense Ratio = Total Expenses / Average Fund Size × 100%
```

### Unit Operations
```
For Unit Split 1:4:
- Units after split = Units before split × 5/4
- NAV per unit after split = NAV per unit before split × 4/5

Income Distribution: Ex-NAV = Cum-NAV - Distribution
```

## Key Concepts

### Unit Trust Characteristics
- Pooled investment vehicle for retail investors
- Professional fund management
- Diversified portfolio
- Liquid investment (buy/sell within 7 days)
- Transparent pricing
- Regulated by Securities Commission Malaysia

### PRS Features
- Voluntary retirement savings scheme
- Tax incentives for contributions
- Default fund allocation based on age
- Protected from creditors
- Restricted access to Sub-Account A
- Once-yearly switching allowed

### Investor Categories
- **Sophisticated Investors:** Net assets >RM3m (individual) or >RM10m (corporation)
- **Wealthy Investors:** Different thresholds
- **Angel Investors:** High-net-worth individuals
- **Premium Investors:** Other categories

## Withdrawal and Redemption

### UTS Redemptions
- No restrictions
- Proceeds within 7 business days
- May incur exit fees
- Can switch between funds
- Cooling-off period for new investors (6 business days)

### PRS Withdrawals

**Retirement Age (55+ or as specified):**
- Full access to all accounts
- No tax penalties

**Pre-Retirement (Sub-Account B):**
- Subject to 8% tax penalty
- Once per year allowed
- Exceptions for hardship
- Healthcare and housing purposes

**Exemptions from Tax Penalty:**
- Permanent departure from Malaysia
- Death
- Permanent total disablement
- Serious disease or mental disability
- Healthcare purposes (self/family)
- Housing purposes (purchase/build/repay)

## Important Definitions

### NAV (Net Asset Value)
The net value of a fund's assets available to unit holders

### Unit Price
The price at which units are bought or sold; calculated as NAV per unit

### Service Charge
An upfront fee charged on investment at the time of purchase

### Cooling-Off Period
A 6 business day period for new investors to reconsider and get a refund

### Switching
Moving investment from one fund to another within the same provider

### Redemption
Selling/withdrawing investment units back to the fund

### Distribution
Payment to unit holders of fund income or capital gains

### MER (Management Expense Ratio)
Annual cost of holding units expressed as a percentage of fund size

### Vesting
The process of units becoming unconditionally owned by employee
